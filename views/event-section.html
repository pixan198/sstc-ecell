March 2019

Awareness drive on Oil and Gas Start-up initiative

On 31 st march 2019 E-CELL SSTC Bhilai organized anawareness drive On “Oil And Gas Start up initiative “.The event was sponsored by KIIT Technology Business Incubator (KIIT-TBI).

KIIT TBI (Technical Business Incubator, Bhubaneswar) is a well-recognised scientific and industrial research organization by DSIR and one of the leading and prominent incubator cells of nation. In order to promote entrepreneurship and to boost innovation KIIT TBI being the implementing partner along with renowned Oil and Gas Public Sector Undertakings (PSUs) is providing a holistic platform to the budding entrepreneurs and innovative brains. It is providing a common portal for short listing of start-ups for funding (Rs. 5-100Cr to individual start-ups) by Oil and Gas PSUs to engage in incubation support/consultancy services.

March 2019

Visit to 36 Inc

On 11 march 2019 E-Cell SSTC organized a trip to 36-Inc Raipur,Chhattisgarh. 36Inc, as a business Incubator and Accelerator ,welcomes any start-up aspiring to develop their ideas and turn into reality or desired to scale an existing business.36Inc as a business incubator and accelerator help start-ups and already existing companies to develop by providing services such as fund support, management, mentorship, co-working space, technological assistance or everything that is required for a business to grow.

The objective of the trip was to make the student aware of the various facilities offered by 36-Inc to the budding start-ups and to give them a chance to interact with the already established start- ups under the aegis of 36-Inc.

One Dollar Venture

This was a fun event in which participants had to Craft a product or service under a specified budget and had to create profit out of it. This event gave them a chance to be an entrepreneur for a day and enhance the marketing and strategic abilities.

Objective :-

1. This is a way to experience the rush and thrill for a short span of time . 2. candidate have an opportunity to became an entrepreneur for a few hours and set up our very first venture.

How it works:-

1. Candidate use a small initial investment to turn them into entrepreneurs! 2. Candidate have only 3 hours to form and run their venture! 3. This is a team activity. You can form teams with your friends; but remember you need to work well with them.
